Prototype #1

Challenge

How might we foster internal motivation in paid subject matter experts when collaborating with LX designers to design and build a course?

For this first prototype, I sought to reframe a SMEs’ role from content producers to co-designers. To this end, I want to “designing for designers” as Mitchel Resnick says. I started with an outline of how I see this progressing:

  1. Prior to Initial Meeting with subject matter expert (SME), send out question for to gather an idea of motivation, goals, and base knowledge of what LX designers can do for them and their course.
